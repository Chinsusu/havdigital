# Official sources and drafting notes

Research checked on 2026-07-16. These are primary government/legal sources or official ministry explanations. They are drafting anchors, not a representation that this content alone makes the business compliant.

## E-commerce and online seller transparency

- [Law on E-commerce 122/2025/QH15](https://chinhphu.vn/?classid=1&docid=216503&orggroupid=1&pageid=27160), issued 2025-12-10 and effective 2026-07-01.
- [Decree 248/2026/ND-CP detailing the Law on E-commerce](https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?classid=0&docid=218747&pageid=27160), issued 2026-06-30 and effective 2026-07-01.
- [Ministry of Industry and Trade explanation of the new Law and Decree](https://moit.gov.vn/tin-tuc/bo-cong-thuong-pho-bien-luat-thuong-mai-dien-tu-va-nghi-dinh-so-248-2026-nd-cp.html). The official summary emphasizes transparent seller, product/service, price, return/warranty and complaint information.

Drafting impact: use final seller identity, disclose service limits and price/renewal/order terms before acceptance, provide complaint/refund channels, and avoid unqualified marketing claims.

## Consumer protection

- [Law on Protection of Consumers' Rights 19/2023/QH15](https://vbpl.vn/hanoi/pages/vbpq-toanvan.aspx?ItemID=161263), effective 2024-07-01.
- [Decree 55/2024/ND-CP detailing the consumer law](https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?docid=210254&pageid=27160), effective 2024-07-01.
- [Ministry of Industry and Trade summary of the 2023 consumer law](https://moit.gov.vn/tin-tuc/bao-chi-voi-nguoi-dan/mot-so-quy-dinh-moi-tai-luat-bao-ve-quyen-loi-nguoi-tieu-dung-nam-2023.html), including remote transactions and online business responsibilities.

Drafting impact: do not waive mandatory consumer rights; give accurate service information, a usable negotiation/complaint route, and fair remedies for verified non-conformity.

## Personal data

- [Law on Personal Data Protection 91/2025/QH15](https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?docid=214590&pageid=27160), effective 2026-01-01.
- [Decree 356/2025/ND-CP detailing the personal-data law](https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/default.aspx?docid=216387&pageid=27160), effective 2026-01-01.
- [Official National Legal Database full text of Decree 356](https://vbpl.vn/TW/Pages/vbpq-toanvan.aspx?ItemID=187276). Article 5 sets request-handling procedures/timelines; Articles 6–7 address verifiable consent and transfers; Article 12 addresses cloud processing; Article 42 repeals Decree 13/2023/ND-CP from 2026-01-01.

Drafting impact: explain categories, purposes, recipients, retention and rights; do not use pre-ticked consent; build a two-working-day initial response process; contract and map cloud/subprocessor data flows; keep policy statements aligned with actual logs and tools.

## Cybersecurity and electronic records

- [Cybersecurity Law 116/2025/QH15](https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?classid=1&docid=216499&pageid=27160&typegroupid=3), effective 2026-07-01.
- [Law on Electronic Transactions 20/2023/QH15](https://vanban.chinhphu.vn/?classid=1&docid=208421&pageid=27160&typegroupid=3), effective 2024-07-01.

Drafting impact: maintain proportionate abuse/security controls and preserve reliable electronic evidence/acceptance records. The AUP should not be treated as a license to monitor content indiscriminately.

## Invoice operations

- [Official Government explanation of Decree 254/2026/ND-CP on electronic invoices and electronic records](https://xaydungchinhsach.chinhphu.vn/toan-van-nghi-dinh-so-254-2026-nd-cp-ve-hoa-don-dien-tu-chung-tu-dien-tu-119260713164251972.htm), effective 2026-07-01.

Drafting impact: the owner must confirm the legal seller and invoice issuer; do not promise an invoice process the business has not implemented.
