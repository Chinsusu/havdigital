# Owner confirmations required

Only items that materially change accuracy or legal effect are listed here.

## Blocking before accepting online orders

1. **Contracting identity:** legal name/entity type of the seller, registered or business address, enterprise/household-business/tax identifier as applicable, representative/contact details and invoice issuer. Publish the required identity on `/contact`, the footer and/or checkout in a legally sufficient form.
2. **E-commerce classification and filing:** confirm with Vietnamese counsel whether `havdigital.com` is a retail e-commerce platform/website under Law 122/2025/QH15 and Decree 248/2026/ND-CP, and complete any applicable notification, registration or seller-identification requirements.
3. **Order flow:** confirm how acceptance, payment, activation, invoices, tax, renewal and cancellation actually work. The Terms deliberately say auto-renewal applies only when an order expressly says so.
4. **No-refund policy:** confirm that all voluntary refunds, service credits, unused-balance returns and cancellation refunds are disallowed. Confirm the billing owner can still handle billing errors, mandatory legal obligations and payment-provider disputes without promising a voluntary refund program.
5. **Data map:** identify every system/provider receiving website, email, order, payment, proxy/VPS account, support, log and abuse data; document storage location, cross-border flow, role and deletion behavior.
6. **Retention:** confirm systems can enforce the published defaults: 90 days for ordinary technical logs; 12 months for unconverted pre-sales communication; service/support term plus 24 months; abuse/security case plus 24 months; statutory retention for tax/accounting records.
7. **Security and privacy requests:** assign an owner who can acknowledge data-subject requests within two working days and complete them within the statutory process. Document identity verification, access/correction/deletion, consent withdrawal, breach response and provider escalation.

## Blocking before making a formal SLA claim

8. **Monitoring:** identify measured endpoints, locations, polling interval, success criteria, source of truth and incident clock. Until then, keep `/status` noindex and do not display a real-time green state.
9. **99.9 wording:** confirm it remains an operating target and does not create a service-credit, compensation or refund commitment.

## Business-policy confirmations

10. **Age/authority:** approve the requirement that an individual buyer is at least 18 and an organizational buyer is authorized.
11. **AUP enforcement:** confirm HAV can technically limit ports/endpoints, isolate resources, preserve relevant evidence and receive/respond to abuse reports without inspecting more customer data than necessary.
12. **Support mailbox:** confirm `sales@havdigital.com` is monitored for `[PRIVACY]`, `[ABUSE]`, `[INCIDENT]` and `[BILLING]` subjects. Do not publish `privacy@`, `abuse@` or a phone number until they actually work.
13. **Claim definitions:** approve the public definitions already agreed: 10M+ is an estimated reachable pool, “IP sạch” is pre-allocation blacklist screening rather than guaranteed third-party acceptance, and 99.9% is an operating target.
14. **Service resale:** confirm the default rule that resale/open proxy is prohibited unless an order expressly permits it.
15. **Liability and disputes:** the draft limits recovery to direct, proven loss where legally permitted but does not impose a fixed monetary cap. The owner and Vietnamese counsel must decide whether a fair, enforceable cap is appropriate for business customers and confirm Vietnamese governing law/dispute wording for the actual contracting entity without removing mandatory consumer rights.

Record the approver, date and policy version for each item. Re-review after material changes to payment, analytics, infrastructure providers, service locations, logging or customer onboarding.
